Rules & Protocols

Heat Rule

Outdoor training strictly before 8:00am or after 8:00pm while Vegas highs exceed 100°F. No exceptions — heatstroke costs weeks, not minutes.

Physical Grid

Morning calisthenics + nightly technical combat isolation are the daily non-negotiables. One Punch Man numbers are a target, not a day-one requirement — ramp ~10%/week from your tested baseline.

Cognitive Primaries

Weeks 1–13: Spanish primary, math secondary (60–90 min/day). Weeks 14–25: Math primary, Spanish maintenance (60 min/day).

Error Correction

Missed a block? No guilt tax. Compress the next block to the highest-yield minimum and keep moving. The streak resets; the record doesn't.

📖 Method of Loci — Quick Reference
1. Terms: one place = a locus; many places = loci. Latin for "places."
2. Go absurd: normal images get deleted by the brain. Make each scene stylized, oversized, or funny — and give it texture, weight, smell.
3. Long phrases: for anything over ~10 words, anchor one core word in the palace and let your grammar reconstruct the rest live.
4. Clear the dock: the palace is temporary storage. Once Anki confirms long-term retention, let that scene dissolve and reuse the spot.
